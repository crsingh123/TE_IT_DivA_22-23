FAQs - Frequently Asked Questions

What occasions are your cakes suitable for?

Our cakes are suitable for a wide range of occasions, including birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, graduations, and more.

How can I personalize the cake for the recipient?

We offer various options for personalization, such as adding a custom message, choosing a specific decoration, or even creating a completely unique design. You can specify your preferences during the ordering process.

What types of cakes do you offer, and what are the most popular flavors?

We offer a variety of cakes, including chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, lemon, and more. Our most popular flavors are chocolate and vanilla.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions such as gluten-free or vegan cakes?

Yes, we offer gluten-free and vegan cake options. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when placing your order.

How can I place an order for a cake, and what is the lead time?

You can place an order through our website or by contacting us directly. Lead times vary depending on the complexity of the order and our current workload, but we typically require at least 48 hours' notice.

What is the price range for your cakes, and what are the payment options?

Our prices vary depending on the size and complexity of the cake. We accept payment via credit card or PayPal, Gpay,PhonePe, etc.

Can you deliver the cake to a specific location, and what is the delivery fee?

Yes, we offer delivery to most locations. Delivery fees vary depending on the distance and time of day.

What is your policy on cancellations or changes to an order?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for any changes or cancellations to an order. Refunds may be issued in some cases, but are subject to our discretion.

How can I track my order, and what happens if there is an issue with the cake?

We will provide tracking information once your order has been shipped or delivered. If there is an issue with the cake, please contact us as soon as possible so we can address the issue.

Do you offer any additional services such as gift wrapping or card messages?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping and card messages for an additional fee. You can specify your preferences during the ordering process.
